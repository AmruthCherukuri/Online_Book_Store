How to order

Step1:

Get yourself registered and fill up all the necessary information for ease of communication with you in future. Go to www.bookfarm.com , click on Registration tab to login

Or

You can call ON 044-00662 00882 to place an order

Step2:

Once you are registered, Username and Password will send to your registered email-ID, Also you will get the activation link, click on the link or copy-paste in the browser window. Now you can use your username & password to Login for Shopping with (website address)

Step3:

Once you have a login-ID, then login to the website and start ordering process. Select the Product you want to purchase, Click on “Add to Cart”.
